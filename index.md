# Privacy Policy for Azri

**Effective Date: March 30, 2026**

Azri (the "Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Application.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### a. Voice Data & AI Processing
When you use the voice-to-task feature, your recorded audio is sent to **OpenRouter** (our AI processing partner) to be transcribed and analyzed for tasks. This data is used solely for the purpose of extraction and is not stored or reused for training models.

### b. Local Task Storage
Your extracted tasks, notes, and local organization data are stored directly on your device using **React Native MMKV**. Azri does not upload your personal tasks to any external cloud database. Removing the app from your device will delete this data.

### c. Billing and Payments
Azri uses **RevenueCat** and **Apple In-App Purchases** to manage premium subscriptions. We do not collect or store your credit card or financial information; this is handled entirely by Apple's secure payment system.

### d. Performance & Diagnostics
We may collect anonymous technical data (e.g., device type, OS version, app crashes) through **Expo** or **App Store Connect** to help us improve the application for all users.

---

## 2. Information Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share only necessary information with the following service providers to enable core application features:
- **OpenRouter**: To process voice logs into text tasks.
- **Apple**: To process your app purchases and subscriptions.

---

## 3. Data Retention
Our Application retains your task data only as long as you keep the app installed on your device. Once you delete the Application, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

---

## 4. Your Rights
Under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR), you have rights regarding your information, including the right to access, delete, or limit the use of your data. Since your tasks are stored locally, you exercise these rights by simply deleting the tasks within the app or uninstalling the app.

---

## 5. Security
We prioritize the security of your information. By keeping your data on your device (rather than a central server), we eliminate the risk of a centralized data breach.
